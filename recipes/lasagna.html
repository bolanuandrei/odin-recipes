Lasagna Recipe

Description

  • Some meat, pork or chicken

Steps

  1. Boil the Lasagna things
  2. Add them on the oven tray
  3. Add red sauce
  4. Add white sauce
  5. Add Lasagna things
  6. Repeat until finish, and add white sauce at the end
